  1. Delivery Velocity
  2. Test Strategy
  3. Design Integrity
  4. Simplicity First
  5. Technical Debt Boundaries
  6. Dependency Discipline
- Provide guidance that can be referenced by later prompts (increment, design, implement, improve).

You MUST:

- Write for humans first: concise, clear, and editable.
